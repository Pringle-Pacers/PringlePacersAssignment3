Scopes and Limits

In our project to make a workout tracker we have decided that it is within our limits to create a website with user functionality, workout list generator / webpages, BMI tracker and as a last potential job is achievements. If there is any more time as a potential extra add on we were thinking of an admin page that allows admins to add exercises to the list. The admin page could be developed in the future to allow removal of users, addition of achievements etc, but it isn't within the scope of the current release. Other potential PBIs or additions will be chat functionality, a map functionality for the closest gym and being able to book a gym session with a professional trainer.
